This is an LGPL build. It is configured WITHOUT --enable-gpl, and the GPL-only
components (libx264, libx265, libxvid, libxavs2, libdavs2, frei0r,
librubberband, libvidstab, avisynth) are disabled. It is configured with
--enable-version3, which makes the applicable licence LGPL version 3.

The FFmpeg binaries are UNMODIFIED. MediaGrabber runs ffmpeg.exe as a separate
process and does not link any FFmpeg library into itself.

The FFmpeg libraries are shipped as separate DLLs (a "shared" build), so you can
replace them with your own build simply by swapping the files in the bin folder.
No relinking of MediaGrabber is required.

Corresponding source, and the build scripts and configuration used to produce
these binaries, are published as assets on the MediaGrabber release that ships
this build:

  https://github.com/Chaos-dwg/MediaGrabber-Releases/releases

and upstream at:

  FFmpeg source : https://github.com/FFmpeg/FFmpeg  (commit 998de74adf)
  Build scripts : https://github.com/BtbN/FFmpeg-Builds
                  (release tag autobuild-2026-07-13-14-11)

Full build details, including the exact ./configure line and the SHA-256 of the
archive the binaries were taken from, are in legal/FFMPEG_BUILD_INFO.txt. You can
also reproduce the configure line by running: ffmpeg.exe -version
